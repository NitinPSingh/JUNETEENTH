JUNE 19, 1865
CHAPTER TWO

A Voice Across the Regions

⚖️
GENERAL ORDER No. 3

Headquarters District of Texas

The people of Texas are hereby informed that,
in accordance with a proclamation from the
Executive of the United States,

ALL ENSLAVED PERSONS ARE FREE.

This freedom carries absolute equality of rights
before God and law. Go forth — let every soul
in every district of this great city hear
this proclamation.

Let freedom ring from every corner of Galveston.

By order of Major General Gordon Granger
